Heart of Surging Flame

The main reward of this event is the new ★5 Medic Operator, Ceylon. 

You can get her for free by just playing the event missions in Obsidian Festival. 

One important thing to note is that Ceylon can only be had from this event, so play now if you need a reliable Medic in your team!

The event missions are split into 2 types: Main Stage & Fairgrounds. The Main Stage works like a normal mission, 

requiring Sanity to play. Completing them rewards you with Obsidian Festival Tickets that you can use to enter the Fairgrounds 

Missions to get Tokens for the Capsule Machine.

Grani and the Knights' Treasure

Arknights' First event, Grani and the Knight's Treasure, features a set of Event Stages for players to clear and farm. 

The event will be split into two parts. Part 1 begins on February 5th, 16:00 UTC-7 and Part 2 begins on February 12th, 16:00 UTC-7. 

Both parts end on February 19th, 3:59 UTC-7.

Clearing stages will yield "Bounty Coins," the event currency which can be traded in for rewards like Grani's Tokens, High Level Materials, and Furniture.
